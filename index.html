Canoe Slalom

Images

The images above show some olympians in their stride, making their way down the "olympic".

Course

The Olympic is a pool of water that rushes down a course clreating swirls and rapids of water for the olympians to fly down.

Objective

The objective of this sport is to get to the bottom of the course while going through gates without hitting them before going through the finish line.

Obstacles

Gates are two wooden rods that hang down from lines going over the course. Each gate has a number and a colour. The number reffers to the order that you go through and the colour is to do with direction you go through the gate. Green being down through the gate and red being going up through the gate. Up is up against the flow of the water, down being down with the flow of the water.
