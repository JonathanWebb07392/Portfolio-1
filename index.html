Canoe Slalom

Images

The images above show some olympians in their stride, making their way down the "olympic".

Course

The Olympic is a pool of water that rushes down a course clreating swirls and rapids of water for the olympians to fly down.

Objective

The objective of this sport is to get to the bottom of the course while going through gates without hitting them before going through the finish line.

Obstacles

Gates are two wooden rods that hang down from lines going over the course. Each gate has a number and a colour. The number reffers to the order that you go through and the colour is to do with direction you go through the gate. Green being down through the gate and red being going up through the gate. Up is up against the flow of the water, down being down with the flow of the water.

Reasons to Start

Fitness

Canoeing is an Low impact sport which means it is easy on your joints so it is all about the paddle and how much you are willing to exercise. There are a lot of techniques to learn so it is always intreasting as there is always more to learn. Some of the techniques being skulling in which you drag your paddle back and forth along the side of your boat to bring you side ways.

Easy

The some of the images that have been shown are the people at the peak of this sport going down fast moving water, this isnt neccersary. Most of our members go to a slow moving river and take their kid down for a paddle. Getting started in coneing has a very low learning curve as you don't need to know much to get the boat moving and to have fun.

Club

We have a club based in London where we have all range of abilities, we have try out nights on the first Wednesday of the month where you can have a go in one of our boats and you will have an instructor leading the group so that everyone is safe as you learn. So if you are unsure come down to our trial session where we provide the kit and you just need to bring the fun.

Events

Some people want the extra thrill of competing agaisnt other paddlers all over the country. There are multiple competions hosted at vairied places where you can have the oportunity to compete.
